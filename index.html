The NLP Journey

How we got from counting words to conversing with machines — told through 15 small programs you can run, plus concept pages for the era that outgrew the laptop.

Each stop below is a self-contained explainer. The Runnable ones come with commented code and a deep-dive README; the Concept ones cover the frontier era, defined by a scale no laptop can reach. They are arranged in rough historical order — because each technique was a direct response to the limits of the one before. Start anywhere, or read the full story in OVERVIEW.md.
